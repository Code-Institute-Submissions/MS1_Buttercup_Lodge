Buttercup Lodge

Celebrate, chill out, or relish in a little of both. The Buttercup Lodge, nestled in rural Donegal is a perfect blend of a boutique and luxury bed and breakfast, providing unique style and exceptional service in elegant surroundings. This one-of-a-kind lodge in Donegal is well thought out from the guests' point of view by providing a modern aesthetic built around an ancient form.

Located just 20 minutes from Donegal Town and 2 miles from North Atlantic Coast, the Buttercup Lodge in Ireland is an oasis, tucked away in a tranquil setting, surrounded by fabulous woodland and the sounds of craic agus ceoil from The Ivy Manor Inn, Buttercup Lodge is a must stay on your visit to Donegal.
